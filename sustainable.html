What is Fast Fashion?


Fast fashion is clothing that is produced in masses to keep up with the ever-changing trends. The goal of fast fashion is just what the name suggests– to produce it fast. Because it is made quickly in large quantities, the clothing is cheap. However, cheap and mass-produced clothing does not have the best quality, hence lacking sustainability.


The Dark Side of Fast Fashion

Although fast fashion may sound like an ideal way to purchase clothing, it has many downfalls. It is harmful both environmentally and ethically. Reasons to re-think fast fashion are-



Environmentally Disastrous:

According to the UN Alliance for Sustainable Fashion, fast fashion is responsible for 2-8% of the entire world’s carbon emissions. The biggest issue with fast fashion is waste. Fast fashion keeps up with ever-changing trends, leading to productions of poor quality clothing that is not meant to last. As a result, consumers only wear a clothing item a few times before throwing it away, contributing to the “waste”. Fast fashion is also responsible for water wastage at alarmingly high levels.


The Exploitation of Workers :

On the ethical side, factory workers for fast fashion companies are often subjected to less than liveable wages. Because the cost of the clothing is so cheap to sell, workers often make a fraction of that price, leading to many of them living below the poverty line. In some cases around the world, major fast-fashion corporations have been accused of providing inhumane work environments for their employees. These big companies exploit their workers. Inhumane practices like child labour is also practiced by some fast fashion brands.


Fast Fashion Can Possibly Cost You More Than “Real” Clothing:

Sure, buying fast fashion can be extremely affordable, but it’s important to think about the quality of the clothing that is being purchased. When a consumer purchases a shirt for Rs.70, it may not hold up for long, the customer throws it away and buys the same shirt again .The same scenario repeats itself and the customer ends up buying a RS.250 high-quality shirt which is more than likely to last longer, and reduces the waste to a minimum.

Sustainable Fashion: A Response to Fast Fashion

Sustainable fashion is clothing, shoes, and accessories that are manufactured, marketed and used as sustainably as possible, taking into account both environmental and ethical aspects. Sustainable fashion is clothing that is produced with a focus on being environmentally friendly. Many sustainable fashion brands also focus on being ethical as well, as the two go hand in hand.


Environmental Benefits of Sustainable Fashion

The main difference in fast vs. sustainable fashion is the amount of waste that each one produces. Sustainable fashion decreases waste by making clothing that is significantly of better quality. Lesser waste leads to both less water pollution and fewer carbon emissions during production. Some sustainable companies also work to reduce waste during production and even collect data to determine better ways to recycle and reuse materials.


Ethical Benefits of Sustainable Fashion

Unlike fast fashion, sustainable fashion focuses more on ethics as well as the environment. They promote healthier work environments by taking extra measures to ensure safety and provide liveable wages to their workers.

Major sustainable companies have verified high standards in both their social and economic performance. This is a major step to reducing inequality and poverty rates by providing opportunities through their business. How wholesome is that!


Significant ethical benefits-

• Providing humane working conditions in their communities which also includes developing areas of India.

• Paying liveable wages to their factory workers to reduce poverty rates and eliminate child labour.

• Give back to the community by working for and supporting the local area.

• Partner with companies that share similar values to their own to support the growth of ‘ethical companies’ as whole.


How Can I Support Ethical Companies?

SUPPORT SMALL-SUPPORT ETHICAL-SUPPORT SUSTAINABLE


Simple. Support ethical companies by consuming what they have to offer. By purchasing their products or services, you are allowing that company to stay in business, giving value to their hard work and determination and ultimately doing good for your community.


Support small businesses , buy from local sellers and contribute towards a sustainable future.


Supporting ethical companies helps encourage brands to become lifestyles. Beyond just fashion, they attempt to educate the public on important issues in their community and around the world. Promote small businesses, non-profit organizations that share their ethical beliefs. Purchasing from an ethical company can help bring its popularity above fast fashion in the future.

Sustainable fashion is the future! If we want our future generations to live in a healthy and ethical climate , we have to work towards a sustainable future ,to which sustainable fashion is a huge step.
